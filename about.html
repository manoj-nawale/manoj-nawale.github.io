About

I’m a data-driven engineer with a Mechanical Engineering background (SPPU Pune) and an M.Sc. in Production & Operations Management (KIT). I build decision support systems for industrial planning — the kind that stays reproducible, auditable, and useful under real-world mess.

How I work

  • Clarify the decision (what changes if we’re right vs wrong?)
  • Make runs reproducible (inputs/outputs/versioning, no mystery)
  • Explain the why (sensitivity, drivers, scenarios)
  • Ship (clean interfaces, readable code, practical artifacts)

Personal bits (short, human, not cringe)

I like solving stubborn problems, building systems that don’t break when scaled, and keeping things simple enough to explain on a whiteboard. Outside work: fitness, tinkering, and turning “that’s impossible” into “cool, watch me.”
